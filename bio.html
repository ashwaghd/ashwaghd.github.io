Bio

Locally Experienced

Alex Callister is a handyman with years of experience in the field of construction. Before serving a two year mission in Santiago Chile, he worked for various construction companies gaining experience and learning the trade.

His technique and attention to detail is second to none, and his speed and efficiency are on a level of their own! When leaving his last job, his boss was sad to see him go because of his quality of work.

Alex grew up curious by nature, with an apparent talent with his hands. He has always loved learning new skills, and years of experience has built a repertoire of abilities!

Alex's Family is originally from Arizona, but moved to Saint George while he was in 3rd grade. He is the second oldest of six children; a brother with his own business, and four little sisters that he adores. Each have their own creative passions as well!
