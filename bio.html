Bio

Locally Experienced

Alex Callister is a handyman with years of experience in the field of construction. Before serving a two year mission in Santiago Chile, he worked for various construction companies gaining experience and learning the trade. His technique and attention to detail is second to none, and his speed and efficiency are on a level of their own!

Alex's Family is originally from Arizona, but moved to Saint George while he was in 3rd grade. He is the second oldest of six children, each with their own creative passions as well!
